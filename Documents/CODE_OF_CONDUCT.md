# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the **Contributor Covenant**.

## Our pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful, courteous, and collaborative
- Provide constructive technical feedback
- Zero tolerance for harassment or personal attacks

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainer via the repository contact information.
